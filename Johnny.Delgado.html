Johnny Delgado

Biography

Johnny was born in San Antonio, TX on January 13th 1985. He graduated from high school in 2003. In 2009 he joined the United States Marine Corp. He was honorably discharged in 2014 as a Sergant of Marines and went on to work for Jon Wayne Service Company. On January of 2020 he enrolled in Codeup.

Military Career

In February of 2009 Johnny enlisted in the United States Marine Corps. Within the first year of his enlistment he earned the rank of Lance Corporal. While in his Marine Occupation school he was given the billet of class leader and was put in charge of his peers. In 2011 he was promoted to the rank of Corporal and was enrolled in Corporal leadership course while deployed in Japan. In 2013 he was promoted to the rank of Sergant and was honorably discharged in 2014.
